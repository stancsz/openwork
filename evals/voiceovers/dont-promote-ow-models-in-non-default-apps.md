# dont-promote-ow-models-in-non-default-apps — Custom Den deployments do not promote OpenWork Models

1. On the default OpenWork Cloud server, users who are not subscribed still see the existing OpenWork Models recommendations and subscribe actions.

2. The model picker continues highlighting OpenWork Models for eligible users, with Sign in or Subscribe based on authentication.

3. Now I switch the desktop app to a custom Den URL.

4. The model picker shows only models available from that Den—there are no OpenWork Models promotional rows or banners.

5. Across startup, status bar, AI settings, provider setup, onboarding, welcome, and first-task flows, OpenWork Models promotions are also absent.

6. Signing into an organization on the custom Den does not restore those promotions; organization-provided models still appear normally.
