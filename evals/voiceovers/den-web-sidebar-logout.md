# den-web-sidebar-logout — Organization controls stay accessible on long dashboard pages

1. I’m logged into Den Web on the dashboard, where the sidebar shows my workspace navigation.

2. As I scroll through a long dashboard page, the sidebar’s organization switcher and logout control remain fixed at the bottom of the viewport.

3. Even at the middle of the page, I can immediately open the organization menu without scrolling to the page footer.

4. The menu remains fully accessible, letting me switch organizations or log out from anywhere on the dashboard.
