# paste-signin-code — Claim a prepared workspace without a deep link

1. I’ve claimed the workspace in the browser. OpenWork gives me two ways to continue: open the desktop app directly, or copy a one-time sign-in code. Because I’m using the development build, I copy the code, switch to Demo A, and choose Paste sign-in code.

2. I paste it once, and Demo A signs me into the workspace as the owner.
