# download-light-card — Light-mode OpenWork downloads

1. On the download page, OpenWork now presents one polished light card: a clean white surface, a subtle version pill, and a short explanation of what the desktop app does after sign-in.

2. The same card lists every desktop build in one place — Apple Silicon and Intel for macOS, x64 and ARM64 installers for Windows, plus AppImage and tar.gz downloads for both Linux architectures.

3. After the page hydrates, the card recognizes the visitor's operating system and adds a small green Detected badge to the matching platform column, so the right choice is obvious without hiding the others.

4. On an Apple Silicon Mac, detection goes deeper than the OS: the macOS column calls out Apple Silicon and the Apple Silicon installer is promoted with a clear “For your device” tag.

5. On a Windows 11 ARM64 PC, the card recommends the ARM64 installer while every other download remains visible, proving the detection is guidance rather than a gate.
